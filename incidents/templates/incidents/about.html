Incident and Report Management System

Empowering Community Safety, Enhancing Police Efficiency

The Rwanda National Police Incident and Report Management System is a powerful tool designed to strengthen the bond between the police and the community. This innovative system offers a user-friendly platform for citizens to:

  • Report Incidents Easily: Quickly and securely submit reports of crimes, accidents, and other incidents.
  • Track Progress: Monitor the status of your reports and receive timely updates.
  • Contribute to Safer Communities: Play an active role in maintaining public safety by reporting suspicious activity.

For law enforcement officers, the system provides:

  • Efficient Case Management: Streamline the investigation process and improve response times.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Utilize advanced analytics to identify trends and allocate resources effectively.
  • Enhanced Collaboration: Facilitate information sharing and coordination among different police units.

By leveraging technology, the Incident and Report Management System empowers both the public and the police to work together in creating a safer Rwanda.
