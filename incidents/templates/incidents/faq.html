Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Incident Reporting and Management System?

The system allows users to report incidents online, track their progress, and assist law enforcement in creating a safer community.

Who can use this system?

Any registered user can report incidents and access various services. Law enforcement officers use it for case management and analysis.

How do I report an incident?

Once logged in, navigate to the 'Report Incident' page, fill in the necessary details, and submit the form.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we prioritize your data's security with modern encryption and secure authentication methods.

How can I track my reported incidents?

You can track your reported incidents by logging in and viewing the 'Incident History' section in your profile.
